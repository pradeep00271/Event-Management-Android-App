About:


An entrepreneurship club founded mainly to encourage young pioneers and startup enthusiasts.Capitalise is a small yet vital limb of ceg.A wing of the entrepreneur development cell of CEG, capitalize enables the interested to take informed decisions to bring in successful entrepreneur and students on a common platform. Further, the club motivates the members on various dimensions of industry to nurture the latent entrepreneurial talent.
